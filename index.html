At a Glance

The fast read

What it is

A website that works like an app. Open it once in your phone's browser, save it to your home screen, and it behaves like any other app — even offline. The next step is packaging it for the Google Play Store and Apple App Store so attendees find it the way they find every other app.

Who's building it

Community technologists using this project to gain real-world experience while solving a real community problem. The build is led by an experienced architect and hosted by Digital Ally Project. This is not paid workforce development — it is community service that builds skills.

What the nonprofit owns on July 17, 2026

A working app and its source code, fully documented, free to use forever under the Apache 2.0 license. All festival data in a portable format. A maintainer's guide for what to keep running year-round and what to refresh next June. No license fees, ever.

Most time-sensitive

Schedule the meeting with Jen — Azure nonprofit credits ($2,000/yr, auto-renewed, currently not realized) and the Zeffy API key both flow through this meeting. Without them, the donation pipeline stalls. See Question 3.

Install on your device

Add this to your home screen

This site can run like an app on your phone — full-screen, offline-capable, no app store required.

iPhone or iPad (Safari)
  1. Tap the Share button (square with an up-arrow) at the bottom of Safari.
  2. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  3. Tap Add in the top-right.
Android (Chrome)
  1. Tap the three-dot menu in the top-right of Chrome.
  2. Tap Install app (or Add to Home Screen).
  3. Tap Install to confirm.
Desktop (Chrome or Edge)
  1. Look for the install icon in the address bar (a small monitor with a down-arrow).
  2. Click it, then click Install.

Heads up before you uninstallTo remove this app later, do not uninstall it from Windows Apps & Features — that can accidentally remove the underlying browser. Instead, open edge://apps/ (Edge) or chrome://apps/ (Chrome) and remove it there. On phones, long-press the home-screen icon and tap Remove.
